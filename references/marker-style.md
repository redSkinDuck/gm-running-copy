# Inline Marker Style

Use Chinese full-width markers so additions fit naturally inside Chinese modules and are easy to search.

## Required Markers

`【NC处理】`
: General GM-side judgment. Use for practical handling, personal running preferences, and "how I would run this" notes.

`【可朗读】`
: Text the GM can directly read aloud to players.

`【信息控制】`
: What to reveal now, what to hide, what to foreshadow, and when to disclose hidden truth.

`【线索】`
: Clue content, clue direction, backup clue source, and whether it is a critical clue.

`【共创】`
: Details that can be delegated to players: costume, prop, memory, relationship, song, symbol, room detail, fighting style.

`【机制提示】`
: System-facing rules, rolls, DCs, SAN checks, madness checks, combat rules, resources, rest logic, and failure handling.

`【偏航处理】`
: What to do if players skip, stall, split, break assumptions, refuse hooks, over-investigate, or solve things early.

`【删改建议】`
: Suggested deletion, compression, replacement, or reordering. Do not silently rewrite authorial intent.

`【节奏提示】`
: Pacing instructions: slow down, speed up, raise tension, give a breather, cut exposition, hold a reveal.

`【伏笔回收】`
: Where a detail pays off later, or how to bring a previous detail back.

## Optional Markers

`【战斗调整】`
: Encounter difficulty, enemy tactics, battlefield changes, boss phases, or action economy.

`【难度提示】`
: Overall risk calibration, harsh consequences, or softening/hardening options.

`【安全工具】`
: Content warnings, table boundaries, fade-to-black suggestions, or pre-session consent checks.

`【玩家提醒】`
: Player-facing reminders the GM may give out of character.

`【开场准备】`
: Pre-session setup, handouts, character requirements, music, props, or opening framing.

`【结局处理】`
: Ending conditions, epilogues, aftermath, and how to handle partial success or failure.

`【改编方向】`
: System conversion, tone shift, setting change, campaign insertion, or personal rewrite direction.

## Style Rules

- Put markers near the original passage they support.
- Keep notes actionable; avoid abstract literary commentary unless it affects play.
- Use natural prep-note language after the marker.
- Use one marker paragraph for one job.
- If rewriting a passage, keep the original nearby or mark the rewrite as a suggestion.
- For read-aloud text, write in a voice the GM can actually speak at the table.

## Examples

```text
【NC处理】这里的 PC 性格绑定太强，建议只保留审卡底线，不要直接要求玩家照着原文演。
```

```text
【共创】如果玩家想拿一件演出服，让玩家自己描述服装风格，并把它当作后续舞台场景的视觉伏笔。
```

```text
【偏航处理】如果两名 PC 坚持一起进电梯，允许成功，不要用失败惩罚打断；可以转成一次亲密对话或短暂互相保护的场景。
```

```text
【信息控制】这条线索可以暗示 PC1 的身份异常，但不要直接揭示真相。让玩家先把怀疑落在“记忆被篡改”或“过去发生过事故”上。
```
